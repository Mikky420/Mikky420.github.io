Calm Creator Tools Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Calm Creator Tools and the Calm Creator Tools Pinterest Content Manager app.

Who we are

Calm Creator Tools is a small creator/business brand for digital planning, productivity, and creator workflow resources. The Calm Creator Tools Pinterest Content Manager app is used to manage Pinterest content related to Calm Creator Tools.

Contact: calmcreatortools@gmail.com

What the app does

The Calm Creator Tools Pinterest Content Manager helps prepare, organize, schedule, and review Pinterest content for the Calm Creator Tools creator/business account. It may support Pin draft preparation, Pin titles and descriptions, board organization, content calendar planning, controlled Pin scheduling, and performance reporting for Calm Creator Tools content.

Each Pin is reviewed and selected before publishing. The app is intended for honest creator content management and is not intended to create spam or fake engagement.

Information we may access from Pinterest

When authorized, the app may access Pinterest account, board, Pin, scheduling, and analytics information needed to provide the described content management and reporting functions for the authorized account.

The app does not request Pinterest passwords, session cookies, or login credentials.

How information is used

Pinterest information is used only to provide Calm Creator Tools content management, scheduling, organization, and reporting functions for the authorized account. This may include preparing Pins, choosing boards, reviewing publishing plans, and understanding performance of Calm Creator Tools Pins and boards.

What we do not do

The app does not scrape Pinterest, sell Pinterest data, share Pinterest API information with third parties, automate fake engagement, require or incentivize engagement, follow or unfollow accounts automatically, send automated messages or comments, collect Pinterest credentials, test rate limits, bypass Pinterest restrictions, or provide competitor benchmarking or platform-wide insights.

The app is not intended for children under 13.

Data storage

The app is designed to use Pinterest access only for the authorized account and only for the functions described in this policy. Local planning files may contain content drafts, content calendars, performance notes, and reporting summaries for Calm Creator Tools. Pinterest API credentials must remain private and should not be shared.

Data sharing

Calm Creator Tools does not sell Pinterest information or share Pinterest API information with third parties. Information may be shared only with the authorized account owner or when required by law.

User control

The authorized Pinterest account owner controls whether the app has access. Access can be removed through Pinterest account settings or by revoking app/API authorization where available.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated if the app purpose, data practices, or legal requirements change. The updated date at the top of this page will show when the policy was last changed.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Calm Creator Tools at calmcreatortools@gmail.com.
